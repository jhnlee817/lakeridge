Welcome to Lake Ridge Music Institute (LRMI) where you can get formal music training with well-educated and degreed teachers. Our faculties are professionally trained musicians and they teach only in their speciality. We offer high quality music education to students of all ages, from preschoolers to adults.

Individuals have different learning capability as well as different learning style, and therefore music lesson should be designed for one's needs in a particular way. Unlike other learning programs, true education of music becomes when children enjoy and feel the joy of music, and our music school is the right place to start. Our mission is to provide highest quality music lessons as well as to provide optimal learning environment. Now it's your chance to allow your children to explore the all the potential that music has to offer.
